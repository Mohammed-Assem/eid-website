Mohamed Assem

Eid Mubarak!

May this Eid bring happiness and peace to you and your family!

Eid is a time of celebration, gratitude, and unity. Families come together to pray, share meals, and give to those in need.

Eid Prayer Times

  • Fajr: 5:30 AM
  • Dhuhr: 12:30 PM
  • Asr: 4:00 PM
  • Maghrib: 6:45 PM
  • Isha: 8:00 PM

Eid Traditions

  • Wearing new clothes
  • Giving and receiving gifts (Eidi)
  • Spending time with family and friends
  • Sharing meals with loved ones
  • Helping the less fortunate through charity

Eid Greetings in Different Languages

  • Arabic: Eid Mubarak (عيد مبارك)
  • Turkish: İyi Bayramlar
  • French: Bonne fête de l'Aïd
